How to Compress PDF Without Losing Quality

Large PDF files can be difficult to email or upload. However, compressing a PDF should not reduce image clarity or text sharpness.

Why PDF Compression Reduces Quality

Heavy compression lowers image resolution and removes embedded data. The key is using optimized compression instead of extreme reduction.

Best Way to Compress PDF Safely

Use an online PDF Compressor that balances file size and clarity. Choose medium compression for best results.

Tips to Maintain Quality

  • Do not over-compress scanned PDFs
  • Avoid repeated compression
  • Keep original copy as backup
